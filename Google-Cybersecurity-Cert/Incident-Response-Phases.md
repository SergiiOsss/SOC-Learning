# 🛡️ Incident Response Playbook: 6 Phases

1. **Preparation:** Setting up tools (SIEM, Kali) and documenting procedures.
2. **Detection & Analysis:** Identifying the breach via logs and alerts.
3. **Containment:** Isolating affected systems to prevent further damage.
4. **Eradication & Recovery:** Removing threats (malware) and restoring services.
5. **Post-incident Activity:** Learning from the event (lessons learned).
6. **Coordination:** Reporting and information sharing with the team.

*Key Principle: Consistency. Every analyst follows the same steps for the same alert.*
