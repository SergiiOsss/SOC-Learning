# Security Audits & Compliance 🛡️📑

## 🎯 Goals of an Audit
- Ensure IT practices meet industry standards.
- Identify vulnerabilities and areas for growth.
- Maintain a strong security posture.

## 🗂️ Control Categories to Assess
- **Administrative:** Policies and procedures.
- **Technical:** Firewalls, encryption, and access controls.
- **Physical:** Hardware security and facility locks.

## 📋 Audit Checklist Progress
1. [x] **Define Scope:** Monitoring my local Kali environment and GitHub repository.
2. [x] **Risk Assessment:** Evaluating the risk of credential theft (solved with PAT tokens).
3. [ ] **Conduct Audit:** Reviewing firewall rules and log monitoring.
4. [ ] **Mitigation Plan:** Strategy to reduce identified risks.
