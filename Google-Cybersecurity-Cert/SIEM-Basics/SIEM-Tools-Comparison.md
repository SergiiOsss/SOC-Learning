# Industry Leading SIEM Tools 🛠️🛰️

## 🏛️ Self-Hosted (On-Premise)
- **Tool:** Splunk Enterprise.
- **Why:** Full physical control over data.
- **Management:** Internal IT department handles the infrastructure.

## ☁️ Cloud-Hosted
- **Tool:** Splunk Cloud.
- **Why:** No need for internal server maintenance. Accessible via internet.
- **Management:** Managed by the vendor (Splunk).

## ⚡ Cloud-Native
- **Tool:** Google Chronicle.
- **Why:** Built *in* the cloud for maximum scalability and speed.
- **Management:** Fully managed by Google.
