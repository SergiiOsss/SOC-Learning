# 🐧 Extended Guide to Linux Distributions

## 🛡️ Security-Focused Distros
- **KALI LINUX ™:** Debian-based. Industry standard for **Penetration Testing** (identifying vulnerabilities) and **Digital Forensics** (post-attack analysis).
- **Parrot OS:** Debian-based. User-friendly alternative to Kali, pre-installed with security and privacy tools.

## ☁️ Enterprise & General Purpose
- **Ubuntu:** Debian-based. The most popular choice for cloud computing and general Linux administration. High community support.
- **Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL):** Subscription-based (paid). Built for high-stakes enterprise environments with dedicated support.
- **AlmaLinux:** Community-driven, open-source alternative to RHEL/CentOS. Focuses on stability and long-term support for servers.

## 🗝️ Key Definitions
- **Penetration Test:** A simulated attack to find and fix security gaps.
- **Digital Forensics:** Collecting and analyzing data after a breach to determine what happened.
- **Package Manager:** A tool to install, update, and remove software within a distribution.
